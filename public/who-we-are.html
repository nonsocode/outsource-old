Who We Are

About us

Outsource Global is successfully reimagining what it means to be a global leader in business processing outsourcing (BPO) and redefining the term ease-of-doing-business. It provides business operations solutions, including call centers, claims processing and a broad range of other IT-based services to enterprises and government agencies in the United States and Africa. Outsource Global seeks to provide a positive social impact and embraces its responsibility to recruit highly-talented, economically and physically challenged individuals. It offers all employees growth opportunities in a competitive, career-focused workforce.

We have over 700 seats across offices in multiple Nigerian cities where we are able to draw the best highly-educated staff to manage our clients' processes. Our top management is comprised of a team of highly talented international employees who combined bring decades of experience in starting and growing companies.

Outsource Global is deeply committed to growing its operations in the U.S., U.K. and Africa and continues to enhance its presence, scale, capabilities and offerings. We will continue to tap into Nigeria's high-caliber workforce and corresponding IT and business process expertise to provide our clients with world-class, price-competitive and cost-effective solutions.

Nigeria's high-caliber educated workforce, well-developed communications infrastructure, stable business environment and economic attractiveness make it a desirable location in which to base our business and grow our abilities to service customers in the U.S. and across Africa and beyond.

Our Group

Outsource Global is affiliated with BlueTag Group, a federation of inter-related independently run high-technology companies emphasizing long-term capital gains and growth through consistent investment in people, strategy, innovation, institutionalization and products. The companies are focused, professionally run, independent but deriving synergies from each other; empowering us to deliver large and complex ICT/engineering automation projects. Emphasis is on long-term capital gains through consistent investment in people, strategy, innovation, institutionalization, and products.

Other members of the group include:

    PARKWAY PROJECTS

    Parkway Projects Limited is a leading electronic banking & payments solutions development company with a wealth of experience spanning over a decade in deploying e-banking & payment systems across a wide cross section of corporate, government & financial services institutions in Nigeria and 30 other African countries.

    Parkway is the first IT Company in Nigeria to attain the new ISO9001:2008 quality certification placing the company amongst an elite list of professionally run organizations worldwide. Parkway is a CBN licensed mobile financial services company. Parkway’s e-banking systems are fully operational across 10 Nigerian banks, the debit networks & financial switches, the Nigerian Inter-bank Settlement System and used by over 3,000 government agencies, large & SME corporates in Nigeria alone.

    HEALTH STATION

    Health Station is an IT consulting & project management company registered in Nigeria and focused on providing value-added Hospital Management Information & Revenue Assurance Services to public hospitals in Nigeria.

    Extensive success stories in deploying total automation to state & federal Teaching Hospitals including Enugu State University Teaching Hospital (ESUTH), Nnamdi Azikiwe University Teaching Hospital, Nnewi (NAUTH), University of Nigeria Teaching Hospital, Ituku-Ozalla (UNTH); with amazing transformation and bottom line results. HealthStation is an official partner to the Nigerian Medical Association (NMA) for the provision of telemedicine services in Nigeria.

    BLUETAG TECHNOLOGIES

    A vastly experienced ICT consultancy and modern enterprise software & automation systems development company. BlueTag has developed & deployed comprehensive education lifecycle management and e-learning systems to tertiary education institutions including the Institute of Management Technology, Enugu (IMT) & Kwararafa University, Taraba.

    BlueTag has also deployed sophisticated Sales Channel Management System to FMCGs like Nigerian Breweries Plc. In its capacity as technology development company for the group, BlueTag has developed the comprehensive hospital management information system (HMIS) and telemedicine platforms for HealthStation.

    BLUETAG SYNERGY

    BlueTag Synergy Projects Limited is Nigeria’s foremost provider of technical support for the oil and gas sector. With pressure mounting to lower costs, improve capacity and reduce risks, the midstream sector demands operational excellence, especially in Nigeria where this company is registered. In positioning itself as the solution to this need, BlueTag Synergy offers control processes and systems that better manage oil and gas assets, and optimize pipeline transportation.
