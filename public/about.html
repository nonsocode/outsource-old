About the Outsource Group

Who we are

We are a 100% Nigerian group deploying best in class technology to meet the needs of our clients. Our solutions are at the cutting edge of contact center business process outsourcing and we have deep vertical expertise and unique understanding of industry specific needs. We put our clients at the heart of everything we do and deliver robust customer support solutions that span every segment of the customer value chain.

Benefits for partnering with us

The Outsource Group delivers superior customer services year after year. Our clients rank our group as the best in providing satisfaction in every aspect of our operations. We are flexible when it comes to our client’s individual needs. We never take a one-size-fits-all approach. Our executive and management team pride themselves on being available and accessible at all times. No matter what your industry, We will create a cost effective, revenue generating solution that is ideal for you. Thus, our continuously provides ways to improve your business.

Our business plans take the simplest yet most effective routes. Everyone at TheOutsource Company is there for our clients, from the employees right up to the management. We constantly seek ways to be the best third-party provider for our clients and employer for our workers.

No matter what the industry, albeit constantly changing; we offer the right solutions to meet the dynamic demands of each client(s).

Our International Focus

The Outsource Group broad vision sees the world as a potential opportunity. In our utmost respect for other cultures, we are constantly on the lookout for ways to promote financial advancement for our clients, however far-flung they may be. Our treasured employees from diverse demographics in Nigeria are given every chance to exchange opinions, grow in their roles, and leverage their experiences as they provide valued contributions to the organization.
The Outsource Group has set its sights on becoming a regional leader in the business processing outsourcing (BPO) and ITES sector. With a large number of US companies moving their BPO and Contact Centre operations closer to home (near shore) located in a near time zone more convenient to serve American clients, The Outsource Group is taking the opportunity to target the sector and attract more FDI into the country.

Our Focus On Customers

We consider our clients as our partners and have a reputation for offering high-quality services. In our do more approach for customers, The Outsource Group has built a reputation on consistently exceeding our clients’ expectations.

An Ethical Approach

Honesty and ethics is key to business success. We pride ourselves on honesty and transparency with our valued partners out of a desire to pass on this approach.
