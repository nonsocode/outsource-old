CORPORATE SOCIAL RESPONSIBILITY

Our corporate culture is built on youth and women empowerment

Our corporate social responsibility policy is guided by our vision to make Africa the premier outsourcing destination for various sectors of the economy - empowering women and youth for a better tomorrow.

This company was founded in order to prove to ourselves and to the world that Nigeria can become a successful world-class outsourcing destination. In proving this, we wanted to provide employment to hundreds of Nigerians, focusing specifically on women and recent college graduates, who are in need of a means for providing livelihood to their families in a way that would ensure their own professional growth.

At Outsource Global, we believe that there should be direct jobs women and youths can engage in which will be culturally acceptable to the society. Over the years, we have provided an enabling environment for hiring women and educated youths in disadvantaged demographics. Based on this, we saw an opportunity to be a voice for women and youths in empowering them for direct jobs which seek to promote diversity, inclusion and equal opportunities.

Through our corporate social responsibility, we have hired nearly 9,000 educated youths and women across our company.

Outsource Global aims not only to increase our company’s and our clients’ profitability but also to improve the quality of life of our employees and to provide value for businesses. As we look to the future, we seek new ways to make a greater impact on communities by fostering development and opportunity to families around the world.
