Our Community

Outsource Global and its affiliated companies seek to provide a growth-enabled career path in the IT industry for individuals, and women particularly, in Nigeria and elsewhere that helps them to develop the skills, knowledge and financial status to elevate their standard of living and those of their fellow citizens.

It also focuses on recruiting economically and physically challenged individuals and bringing them into a competitive, career-focused workforce. Outsource Global gives back to the community in multiple ways:

  • Donations
  • Facility/asset use
  • Mentorship programmes
  • Partnerships on social-impact driven projects

Through our corporate social responsibility, we have hired nearly 9,000 educated youths and women across our company.
