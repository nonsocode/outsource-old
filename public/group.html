Companies

    PARKWAY PROJECTS

    Parkway Projects Limited is a leading electronic banking & payments solutions development company with a wealth of experience spanning over a decade in deploying e-banking & payment systems across a wide cross- section of corporate, government & financial services institutions in Nigeria and 30 other African countries.

    Parkway is the first IT Company in Nigeria to attain the new ISO9001:2008 quality certification placing the company amongst an elite list of professionally run organizations world-wide. Parkway is a CBN licensed mobile financial services company. Parkway’s e-banking systems are fuhlly operational across 10 Nigerian banks, the debit networks & financial switches, the Nigerian Inter-bank Settlement System and used by over 3,000 Government agencies, Large & SME corporates in Nigeria alone.

    HEALTH STATION

    Health Station is an IT consulting & project management company registered in Nigeria and focused on providing value-added Hospital Management Information & Revenue Assurance Services to public hospitals in Nigeria.

    Extensive success stories in deploying total automation to State & Federal Teaching Hospitals including Enugu State University Teaching Hospital (ESUTH), Nnamdi Azikiwe University Teaching Hospital, Nnewi (NAUTH), University of Nigeria Teaching Hospital, Ituku-Ozalla (UNTH); with amazing transformation and bottom line results. HealthStation is official partner to the Nigerian Medical Association (NMA) for the provision of telemedicine services in Nigeria.
